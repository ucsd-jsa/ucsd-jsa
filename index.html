Looking to practice Japanese?

JSA offers a Language Table every Friday from 5-6pm at Cafe Ventanas to help you practice your Japanese or English. Beginner, intermediate, advanced – all levels are welcome! It’s a great opportunity to practice your speaking while meeting new people who share the same interests as you!

Want more JSA?

JSA Hangouts is a Facebook page where anyone can make casual posts to hang out with their JSA friends outside of our events! Whether you want to go on a boba run, study at Geisel, or go star gazing at Gliderport, this is the place to post your ‘hangout’ and find your crew. Come join us!
